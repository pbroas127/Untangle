Tip 01
Brain
Dump
Everything
Write every thought down. All of it, messy and unfiltered.
Getting thoughts out of your head and onto paper breaks the loop — your brain stops recycling them once they're written.
02
Untangle 03 / 07
Tip 02
4-7-8
Breathing
Inhale for 4. Hold for 7. Exhale for 8. Repeat 3 times.
Your nervous system physically cannot stay in overdrive on that exhale. It's not a trick. It's biology.
03
Untangle 04 / 07
Tip 03
Name It
Out Loud
Say the worry out loud. Literally: "I'm worried about X."
Naming a thought separates you from it. You become the observer, not the thought. That gap is where the spiral stops.
04
Untangle 05 / 07
Tip 04
The
20-Minute
Rule
Can't sleep after 20 minutes? Get up and go to another room.
Come back only when you feel tired. Your brain learns: bed means sleep, not anxiety.
05
Untangle 06 / 07
Tip 05
Tomorrow
Is Not
Tonight
Your brain turns tomorrow's tasks into tonight's emergencies.
Write "deal with tomorrow" next to each worry, close the notebook, and tell yourself: this is not urgent right now.
